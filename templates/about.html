Retinal Eye Disease Detection

Retinal eye disease detection involves using imaging techniques like OCT / fundus photography to capture detailed images of the retina.
These images are then analyzed for signs of diseases like diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration, and glaucoma.
AI algorithms are used to assist in the analysis, helping to detect early signs of these diseases, which can be crucial for timely treatment and preventing vision loss.
